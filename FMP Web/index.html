About Us

Welcome to Shamsi Packages


Shamsi Packages has been a trusted name in the industry for over 35 years. We have one of the widest selection of packaging products online to please merchants and customers alike.

From our economical to high-end gift boxes, we offer packaging solutions to suit every style and every budget, for wholesale or retail.

Our aim is to combine the high quality and great value of our packaging products with exceptional customer service:

Our team of knowledgeable representatives are always available by email or telephone to answer your questions, or at the Noble offices to show you our extensive selection of packaging and displays.
